Performances around the world

  • 2012- 2015

    Bangalore

    As a Bangalore-based organization, a majority of our efforts have been concentrated in the city. These include fourteen productions in performances spaces such as Attagalatta and Rangasthala, as well as educational instiutions such as the National Centre for Biological Sciences and the United Theological College. Through our partnership with Visthar: Academy for Peace and Justice Studies, we have helpd training workshops and performances for students and faculty of Davidson College, Concordia College, Union Presbyterian Seminary and Columbia Seminary.

  • April 2014

    Minnesota

    Concordia College, Minnesota hosted a dramatic reading of the play in the Barry Auditorium. The performance was directed by David Wintersteen, Director of the Concordia College Theatre. Playwright Sudhir Selvaraj spent the week leading up to the performance teaching in classes, addressing public lectures and hosting a discussion post the reading to a full house.

  • 2014

    London

    Playwright Sudhir Selvaraj led workshops for students from King's College London, London School of Economics and School of Oriental and African Studies and hosted performances in the city. With each performance came working with participants to adapt the script to meet the interests of the participants.
