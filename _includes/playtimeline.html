Performances around the world


  • 2013 - 2014

    London

    Playwright Sudhir Selvaraj led workshops for students from King's College London, London School of Economics and School of Oriental and African Studies and hosted performances in the city. Each performance entailed working with the participants to adapt the script to meet their interests.

  • April 2014

    Minnesota

    Concordia College, Minnesota hosted a dramatic reading of the play in the Barry Auditorium. The performance was directed by David Wintersteen, Director of the Concordia College Theatre. Playwright Sudhir Selvaraj spent the week leading up to the performance teaching in classes, addressing public lectures and also hosted the post-reading discussion to a full house.

  • 2014 - 2015

    Bangalore

    Our efforts have been conccentrated in Bangalore. These include fourteen productions in performance spaces, educational instiutions and community clubs. Through our partnership with Visthar: Academy for Peace and Justice Studies, we have helped conduct workshops and performances for students and faculty of Davidson College, Concordia College, Union Presbyterian Seminary and Columbia Seminary.
